RIO 2016 OLYMPIC GAMES

PODIUM REVIEW: Most Decorated Countries


The 2016 Olympic Games took place in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, and hosted 306 athletic competitions, collectively featuring 11,237 athletes from 206 countries. Despite the odds, the United States represented well, winning a total of 121 medals, which is 1.73 times the medals earned by the second most decorated nation, China.
