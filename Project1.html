2016 Summer Olympic Games: Podium Review

The 2016 Olympic Games took place in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, and hosted 306 athletic competitions, collectively featuring 11,237 athletes from 206 countries. Despite the odds, the United States represented well by winning 121 medals, 1.73 times the medals earned by the second most decorated nation, China.
